Classic Quick Guacamole

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Ripe Avocados 3
Lime (juiced) 1
Salt 1 tsp
Diced Onion 1/2 cup

Steps:

Step 1: In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.

Step 2: Mix in the onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.

Step 3: Refrigerate for 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately!

Source: AllRecipes

Fluffy Space Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1.5 cups
Milk 1.25 cups
Egg 1 Large
Baking Powder 3.5 tsp

Steps:

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat.
  4. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
